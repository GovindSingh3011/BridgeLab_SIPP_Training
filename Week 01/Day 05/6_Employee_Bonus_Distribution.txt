1. Input: List<Employee>
   Each Employee has: Name, Department, Rating, Salary
2. Process using Streams:
   - Group employees by department.
   - Calculate department salary total.
   - Bonus pool = 10% of department salary.
3. Distribute bonus proportionally based on rating.
   - Higher rating → higher share of pool.
4. Example:
   - IT Department salary = 10,00,000 → Bonus pool = 1,00,000
   - Emp-A (rating 5) → 50,000
   - Emp-B (rating 3) → 30,000
   - Emp-C (rating 2) → 20,000
5. Output: Department-wise bonus distribution report.
End
